Spirulina

Spirulina is a blue- green algae that contains vitamins such as A, B, E, K, and nutrients such as beta-carotene, gamma-linolenic acid, iron, calcium, magnesium, manganese, potassium, selenium, zinc and bioflavonoids . It has high vitamin B12 content, due to which it is often been recommended as a supplemental source of vitamin for vegetarians, who has far-fetched source of dietary vitamin B12. It is an excellent source of vegan protien that is similar to animal protein, but does not contain saturated fats, or residues of hormones or antibiotics that are in some meats. The product is currently being hailed as the ‘‘Superfood’’ of the future because of its exceptional nutritional content. Moreover Spirulina contains risk free protein almost on par with mother’s milk in terms of enzymes and other minerals.
